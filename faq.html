Is this a real place?

Unfortunately, no. This site is created as a university project. But if you have spare money to give away, please let us know and arrange a safe place to meet. As students, we need money to survive, especially during those tough times.


Where can I get shoes?

From a shoe store near you! Or a clothing store that also sells shoes, any is ok. Remember that you need money to buy shoes. Otherwise they won't let you out the store with the shoes you want. If you manage to get out without paying, that's stealing and you will be suspicious.


Where is the store located?

This is currently an online-only store, so we don't have any location set up. The locations may be set up in the future.


Help! I got struck by lightning!

This isn't a question. Also, we aren't medical professionals either, so if that has happened to you, please visit your doctor. Only thing we can do is making you look stylish in your injury/potential death.
